
## How to Integrate

1. At parser output: call `evidence_builder.build_from_account_balance(account, source_file, ...)`
2. Pass `AccountEvidence` object alongside `AccountBalance` through pipeline
3. At each stage (learning, semantic, dictionary), update `evidence.classification_method/confidence`
4. At shadow serialization: call `evidence_serializer.to_shadow_compatible(evidences)`
5. For full preservation: call `evidence_serializer.save_evidence_json(evidences)`
